Admissions

How to Apply

Applications to Dawson College DEC programs are submitted through the SRAM admissions portal (Service régional d'admission du Montréal métropolitain). Applications for the Fall semester typically open in January.

  • Complete Secondary V with Sciences (SN or TS) or equivalent
  • Submit application via SRAM by the March deadline
  • Provide official transcripts and any required supporting documents
  • Await admission decision — typically issued in May
  • Confirm your place by the stated deadline
